AHMED NASIR "NAATTE"

Published on: 31 May 2025, 15:25

Ahmed Nasir "Naatte" – The Coach Who Builds Champions

In the world of Baibalaa, there are few names as respected and revered as Ahmed Nasir, better known as "Naatte." His legacy isn’t just built on wins — it’s built on transformation, vision, and an unwavering belief in discipline and hard work.

Naatte's journey in Baibalaa began as a player, where he carved his name into history by winning back-to-back championships — first with Maziya Sports in 2000, and then with Club Teenage in 2001. But it was as a coach that he truly changed the game.

What sets Naatte apart isn’t just the number of titles he’s won, but how he’s won them. He doesn't chase glory with already established giants. Instead, he uplifts underdogs. He takes new teams — teams with heart — and shapes them into champions. His coaching career is a testament to this remarkable ability.

His last six titles are a story of belief and transformation: Buru Sports Club (2007) TC Sports Club (2011) UN Friends (2013) Kuda Henveiru United (2019) Club O1O (2022) and T.F.T (2025)

Each of these victories was historic — not only because they brought new names to the champion’s list, but because they were led by someone who saw potential in those teams.

According to Naatte, the secret to success is simple: discipline and hard work. It’s a principle he has passed on to every team he’s guided, and it’s what has made him not just a coach, but a mentor and inspiration to players across generations.

Despite all he has achieved, he shows no signs of slowing down. Teams still seek him out — not just for his tactical mind, but for the culture he instills. And as long as he’s coaching, we can expect more surprise champions, more emotional journeys, and more moments of magic.

Naatte also dreams of something bigger — to see Baibalaa reach beyond our borders, included in platforms like the SAFF tournaments and embraced across the region. With his passion and leadership, that dream doesn’t seem too far-fetched.

To the coach who never stops building champions — thank you, Naatte. The Baibalaa community stands with you, excited for whatever comes next.
