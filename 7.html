Ahmed Firaz Moosa "Haandoo" – A True Baibalaa Icon

Ahmed Firaz Moosa, popularly known as "Haandoo," is undeniably one of the legends of Baibalaa. Crowned Mr. Baibalaa not once but twice — in 1434 (2013) with UN Friends and again in 1440 (2019) with Kuda Henveiru United — both times as a champion, Haandoo's legacy speaks for itself. And these aren't the only titles to his name.

With multiple championships across different teams, his career spans an impressive list of Baibalaa powerhouses, including BG Sports Club, Maziya Sports, TC Sports Club, Lorenzo Sports Club, Kuda Henveiru United, and UN Friends. Few players in the game have worn as many elite jerseys and made an impact at each stop.

What sets Haandoo apart is his distinctive style — a rare blend of power, technique, and versatility. Whether attacking or defending, his approach is both effective and entertaining. He brings the full package: strength, awareness, and skill — everything a top-tier Baibalaa player needs.

With over two decades of experience on the sand, Haandoo continues to inspire generations. Here’s hoping we get to witness his magic once again in next year’s tournament. Good luck, Haandoo — a true master of the ring.
